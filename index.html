About LKGainss

20v1 Guy is a nickname given to LKGainss, a Canadian fitness trainer and content creator who went viral on TikTok in early 2025 for posting videos in which he asks why he gets "negative aura and cancelled" for allegedly winning a 20 vs. one fight. The "20v1" videos began in February 2025 when LKGainss documented himself traveling back to his old high school and wrestling with kids, claiming that he was jumped by 20 students while there. LKGainss proceeded to post many videos mentioning the 20v1 altercation, inspiring some fans to tell him he was "dragging" the meme. Previously, in 2023, LKGainss garnered online attention as the "biggest 18 year old in high school" after a comedy video of him beating kids up in a school went viral.

Online History

"Biggest 18 Year Old In High School" Video

On April 30th, 2023, YouTuber tomigains posted a video giving commentary on another video starring LKGainss as the "biggest 18 year old in high school," garnering over 600,000 views in two years. In the video, LKGainss is seen beating up and bullying kids around the school. Tomigains believes the video to be a comedy skit.The original upload of LKGainss' video is no longer available, as his previous account was removed, but reposts have since been reuploaded.

20v1 Guy

On February 4th, 2025, LKGainss posted a video to his Instagram and TikTok pages in which he is shown revisiting his old high school, Ridgemont High School in Ottawa, Ontario. He had been advertising his visit to the high school in previous videos, sparking a joke among his viewers that he would get jumped if he went to the school. The videos of his visit garnered over 144,000 views and 1.9 million views, respectively, in three months.

On February 6th, lkgainss posted a video exclusively to his Instagram page that shows him wrestling a group of students at the high school, garnering over 1.1 million views in three months.

On February 8th, 2025, @lkgainss posted a video to TikTok explaining that he got jumped by 20 people at the school, showing a brief clip of himself purportedly getting jumped by the students, garnering over 1.6 million views in three months. He continued to post numerous videos mentioning the 20v1 over the following days, resulting in some commenters telling him he's "dragging" the meme.

On February 23rd, @lkgainss posted a video to TikTok complaining about people giving him backlash for asking for 100,000 followers for winning the 20v1 and wondering why he gets negative aura for it, garnering over 1.1 million views in three months.

Lkgainss leaned into the meme over the following months. For example, on March 17th, 2025, TikToker @lkgainss posted a video making a joke about the 20v1, garnering over 8.5 million views in two months.

Online Presence

Lkgainss has over 159,000 followers on his @lkgainss Instagram page and 141,000 followers on TikTok. In 2025, lkgainss became the subject of further memes on TikTok. On April 23rd, 2025, TikToker @officialnamour posted a man-on-the-street interview video in which he talks to lkgainss, who makes a 20v1 joke, garnering over 8.1 million views in just over a week.

On April 24th, TikToker @binglzoid posted a video parodying lkgainss, garnering over 1.3 million views in a week.
